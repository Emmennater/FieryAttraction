Rules

  • Don't crash into the sun or asteroids.
  • Shoot RED asteroids for more fuel.
  • Shoot ORANGE asteroids for more ammo.
  • Shoot GREEN asteroids for more health.
  • Survive for as long as possible.
  • Have Fun!
